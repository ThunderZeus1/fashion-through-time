Frequently Asked

A few quick answers before you reach out.

Why aren't more designers / decades shown?

We've curated a small set of designers, eras and iconic garments - clearly not every name is here. Drop us a note via the form if there's a designer or piece you'd love to see featured.

Can I use the images and content for my own project?

The site itself is a non-commercial fan project. All photographs are sourced from Wikimedia Commons under their original licences - check each image's source on Commons for attribution requirements.

How do I report a broken link or wrong information?

Email us at ftt@gmail.com or use the form above. Include the page URL and a short description and we'll fix it quickly.

Will more decades or designers be added?

Yes - we plan to extend backwards into the 1920s and 30s, and add deeper designer profiles. If there's an era or designer you'd like featured, let us know.

Is this site available offline?

Not yet, but a downloadable archive of the museum is on the roadmap. Sign up via the form to be notified when it's ready.
