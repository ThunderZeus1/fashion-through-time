02 - Decades

Featured Decades

Each era left its mark. A few highlights to begin the journey.

1950s

Dior's New Look returned femininity and luxury to a post-war world. Cinched waists, full skirts, and white gloves defined the decade.

Read More

1970s

Bohemian flair, disco glamour, and Yves Saint Laurent's Le Smoking. A decade of bell-bottoms, freedom, and bold silhouettes.

Read More

1980s

Power dressing took over the boardroom. Shoulder pads, bold colour, big hair, and the rise of the supermodel.

Read More

1990s

Minimalism met grunge. Calvin Klein slip dresses, Kate Moss heroin chic, and the era when "less is more" became a manifesto.

Read More

2020s

Sustainability redefines luxury. Genderless silhouettes, Y2K revival, and a generation rewriting the rulebook.
